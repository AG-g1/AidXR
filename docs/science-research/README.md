# Science Research — VR Neurotherapy for Children

Peer-reviewed research supporting VR-based interventions for pediatric ADHD, Autism, and Anxiety. These studies are referenced on the [Science page](../../src/pages/SciencePage.jsx) of the AidXR website.

## Key Statistics

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| ADHD attention improvement (effect size) | Cohen d = -0.68 (p < .001) | Li et al. 2024, JMIR Serious Games |
| ASD emotional skills improvement | SMD = 2.45 (p = 0.03) | Meta-analysis, Research in Developmental Disabilities 2024 |
| ASD social skills improvement | SMD = 1.43 (p = 0.05) | Meta-analysis, Research in Developmental Disabilities 2024 |
| Anxiety reduction (VRET vs. waitlist) | Hedges' g = -0.86 to -1.14 | Meta-analysis, Taylor & Francis 2024 |
| EndeavorRx clinically meaningful change | 68% of parents at 2 months | Akili / The Lancet Digital Health |
| EndeavorOTC attention improvement | 83% of participants | Akili, 2024 |
| VR pediatric safety (ages 4-10) | No noteworthy adverse effects | Systematic review, PubMed 2024 |
